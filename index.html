Cecilia Cavero-Sanchez

Dual Title Ph.D. Student in Political Science and Social Data Analytics, Pennsylvania State University

Welcome to my website! I study how peace is built, sustained, and challenged, focusing on aid, civil society, and dispute resolution through multi-method research. I am a Dual Title Ph.D. Student at Penn State, where I manage the Networks of Influence project at the RIPIL Lab and co-convene the Junior Scholars in Quantitative Conflict Workshop. My research has been supported by the ReCIPE Research Initiative, the ICDS and Department of Political Science at Penn State, and the La Caixa Foundation.
